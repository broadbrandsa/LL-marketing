# Assumptions

This proposal site presents a **directional strategy**. The assumptions below
frame what the plan is — and is not — at the point of presentation.

## Data & access

- **No internal data access.** The plan was authored without access to Liquid
  Lighting's live analytics, ad accounts, Shopify back-end, margin data, or
  historical performance. Nothing here is derived from confidential client data.
- **Source of truth is the written plan.** All strategic content is drawn from
  `BB-LL-LAUNCH-2026-002` and its companion calendar `BB-LL-CAL-2026-001`.
- **No live integrations.** The site is static marketing content. It does not
  connect to Google Ads, Meta, Shopify, Search Console, or any client system.

## Strategy

- **Directional, not final.** Channel mix, campaign structure, cadences and the
  launch sequence represent Broadbrand's recommended approach. They are a starting
  point for collaboration, not a fixed contract of deliverables.
- **Targets are set, not assumed.** The specific CPA target and per-campaign
  budgets are agreed against the confirmed media budget and product margins
  **before** launch. The plan defines *what* is measured and *how* it is acted on;
  the live numbers live in the performance dashboard.
- **Launch-date independent.** Timing is expressed relative to **Launch Day (Day 1)**.
  Once a launch date is confirmed, the companion calendar maps every milestone to a
  real date; the cadence does not change.

## Commercial

- **Investment figures** (R30,000/mo media + R30,000/mo retainer over 3 months)
  are the proposed terms and **exclude VAT**.
- **PPC media spend** is paid directly to the advertising platforms and is fully
  allocated to working ad budget.
- **Spend levels are reviewed** at the end of the 3-month engagement window before
  moving to steady-state.

## Requires validation post-approval

Before launch, the following must be confirmed with the client:

1. Confirmed launch date and the resulting calendar mapping.
2. Agreed CPA target and per-campaign budget splits against real margins.
3. Access to Shopify, Google (Ads, Merchant Centre, Search Console) and Meta
   (Business, Commerce) accounts.
4. Product catalogue and feed readiness for Merchant Centre and Meta Commerce.
5. Brand assets, creative direction and any compliance constraints.
6. Final contact details and the approval contact for the CTA links.
